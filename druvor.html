Röda viner

Nebbiolo

Produces tight and nuanced wine. Can also produce a soft and berry wine. With a significantly taste of acidity and or tannins. Comes with a bride spectrum of scent and taste experience. Grows in Barolo,Bordeaux,Chianti and Priorat.

pinotnoir

Produces Soft and berry wines. They are often light, fresh and have a soft profile, which fits with lighter meals. Comes from regions Bourgognes, Beaujolais and Piemonte.

Merlot

The wines that are grown from merlot can be both soft and berry, but also tight and nuanced wines.

Cabernet sauvignon

The Grape makes fruity and tastefull wines, can have both soft and slim tannines. The wines can often be tight and nuanced aswell.
