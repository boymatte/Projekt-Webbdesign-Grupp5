Information om de olika druvorna

Röda viner

Nebbiolo

Produces tight and nuanced wine. Can also produce a soft and berry wine. With a significantly taste of acidity and or tannins. Comes with a bride spectrum of scent and taste experience. Grows in Barolo,Bordeaux,Chianti and Priorat.

pinotnoir

Produces Soft and berry wines. They are often light, fresh and have a soft profile, which fits with lighter meals. Comes from regions Bourgognes, Beaujolais and Piemonte.

Merlot

The wines that are grown from merlot can be both soft and berry, but also tight and nuanced wines.

Cabernet sauvignon

The Grape makes fruity and tastefull wines, can have both soft and slim tannines. The wines can often be tight and nuanced aswell.

Vita Viner

Chardonny

Most popular regions are France, California and South Africa. It Produces tastes of Lemon zest and green apple when unoaked. But oaked aged is more fuller with buttery finish and taste of vanilla.

Riesling

Biggest regions are Germany and France. Produces a very flowery aromatic taste, very high in acidity. Also common tases are pear, apple, grape and apricot.

Sauvignon Blanc

Most popular areas are New Zealand and France.Tastes very dry, Lime,green apple, and Passion Fruit. Also freshly cut grass. The new Zealand tastes more asparagus, pea-shoots, mango and papaya.

Chenin Blanc

Grown in France, South Africa and USA. From dry to sweer, always high fruit acid. Often tastes of grape, yellow pear, tropical- fruit and honey.
